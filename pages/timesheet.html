📋 How to Use Payroll Calculator
➕
Logging an Entry
Tap the + button (mobile) or Log Entry (desktop sidebar) to record a shift. Enter your start & end time in 15-minute increments, select your break duration, and choose a rate type.
⏱
Rate Types
Choose the type that matches your work:
Normal XP Cash Emergency
Normal — Regular pay; OT kicks in after daily threshold.
XP — Comp time at 1.5× (1h worked = 1.5h credited).
Cash — All hours count as OT at 1× immediately.
Emergency — Call-out OT with a custom $/hr rate.
📅
Day View
The default view shows today's entries, total hours, OT hours, and estimated earnings. Use the ‹ › arrows to navigate days, or tap Today to jump back.
📊
Biweekly Summary
The Biweekly tab shows a 14-day calendar for your current pay period. Tap any day to jump to it. Use the toggle to switch between the current and previous pay period.
🗓
Month Calendar
The Month tab shows every day at a glance. Days with entries show their total hours. A green dot means overtime was logged. Tap any day to open it in Day view.
⚙️
Settings
Set your hourly rate so earnings are calculated automatically. Configure your daily OT threshold (hours before overtime starts) and your pay period anchor date. Use Export / Import to back up your data.
