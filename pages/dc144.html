How to Use DC-144 Field Form
Pick a Form Type
On the dashboard, tap one of the four report tiles to start a new report: A (General), B (HMA Paving), C (Surface Treatments), or D (Piles).
Drafts Save Automatically
Your work is saved to this device every couple of seconds. Watch the Saving… / Draft Saved pill above the bottom action bar. Tap Back to Reports anytime to come back later.
Add Rows and Photos
Use the Add Pay Item / Paving Entry / Application Entry / Pile Entry button to add a row. In each remarks section, tap the + tile to attach a photo from your camera or gallery — captions become part of the Photo Appendix.
Reuse Headers with Templates
Tap Save as Template to store the header (project, contract, contractor, inspector) for next time. From the dashboard, your saved templates appear with a color-coded form-type badge.
Sign Before You Export
Tap Add Signature next to Inspector Name to draw your signature with finger or stylus. It is embedded next to your name on the exported form.
Export to Excel
Tap Export XLSX when you're ready. A review modal shows any missing fields. Confirm to download the official NJDOT DC-144 workbook with your data, signature, and a Photo Appendix tab.
