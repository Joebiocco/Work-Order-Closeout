How to Use Fuel Station Finder
1
Start with Your Location
The page requests your current GPS location when it opens. Use Refresh Location anytime you need a fresh position in the field.
2
Read the Map and Station List
Stations are sorted by distance from you. The pulsing marker shows your location, and selecting a station card centers the map on that station pin.
3
Filter What You Need
Use the fuel filters and Show Closed control to narrow the list and map to the stations that matter for the current trip.
Unleaded Diesel Closed
4
Save Important Stations
Tap the star on a station card to bookmark it. Your My Bookmarks area stays near the top so saved stations are easy to revisit.
5
Directions, Coordinates, and Exports
Use Directions or Google Maps for navigation, copy coordinates from a station card, and export either the visible filtered list or the full station list for Google My Maps.
